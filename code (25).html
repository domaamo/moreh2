About Moreh - عن مريح

Crafting Modern Comfort Since 2025

Founded in Egypt in 2025, Moreh has become synonymous with modern minimalist furniture that combines sleek design with exceptional functionality.

Our name "Moreh" (مريح) means "comfortable" in Arabic, reflecting our commitment to creating furniture that enhances your living space with both style and comfort, all within a coherent minimalist aesthetic.

Transforming spaces with stylish, comfortable, and durable furniture. Your dream home starts here ❤.

Shipping all over Egypt.
